Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve Lithonia, GA and the greater metro area. For long-distance towing please call for availability and quotes.

How do I request emergency towing?

Call (470) 600-2326 for immediate service. For planned or non-emergency moves, use our online quote tool.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and Zelle.

Do you offer roadside assistance without towing?

Yes — jump starts, flat tire changes, lockout service, and fuel delivery are available and may prevent the need for a tow.
